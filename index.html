Features

What is the advantage of an engineering data sharing platform?

For engineering companies

Access to verified supplier catalogues with 3D models and documentation, and information on suppliers of basic components. Search for alternative suppliers and match components by characteristics.

For component suppliers

Publish manufacturer catalogues containing drawings, 3D models and materials required by customers, enabling timely delivery of catalogue updates and product release information to customers.

For engineers and constructors

Integration of CADBase with CAD simplifies the use of 3D models and drawings from supplier catalogues, allowing parts to be added to the CAD workspace, reducing routine work.

For designers and creators

The platform offers storage for engineering projects, CAD files and associated data, along with a thorough version control system. CAD integration enables users to upload data promptly to the platform's storage and retrieve it through CAD.
